Track Descriptions

1. Legio IX Hispana

Evokes the famous Ninth Hispana Legion, showcasing military discipline and the strength of Roman soldiers.

2. Stoicism

Inspired by Roman philosophy, it conveys serenity, reflection, and the pursuit of virtue in the face of adversity.

3. Praetorians

Portrays the Praetorian Guard, with a solemn and strategic tone reflecting their role as protectors of the emperor.

4. Farming

Captures Roman rural life, with melodies evoking work in the fields and agricultural routines.

5. Gladiators

Recreates the intensity of fights in the Colosseum, with the drama and energy typical of gladiators.

6. Senate

Evokes debates and ceremonies in the Roman Senate, with formality and rhythm reflecting the politics of the era.

7. Punic Wars

Transports the listener to the conflicts between Rome and Carthage, with military tension and strategic drama.

8. Saturnalia

A Roman celebration full of joy and festivity, evoking the spirit of the Saturn festival.

9. Mars Praise

Tribute to the god of war, with an epic tone celebrating Rome's military strength and valor.

10. Strength And Honor

Closes the album with a message of power and nobility, highlighting the ideals of Roman strength and honor.
