Roman Empire (I)

Album by Pablo Nuñez Serra | Historical Instrumental Music

← Back to portfolio

Track Descriptions

1. Temple

Recreates the atmosphere of Roman temples, with sounds evoking rituals and religious devotion.

2. Baths

Transports the listener to Roman public baths, showcasing social life and relaxation of the era.

3. Battle

Evokes the chaos and tension of a Roman military clash, with martial percussion and dramatic intensity.

4. Triumphal March

A triumphal march celebrating military victories, with the strength and solemnity of ancient Rome.

5. Markets And Circuses

Recreates the bustle of marketplaces and the excitement of public spectacles, mixing rhythm and local color.

6. Barbarian Attack

Captures the threat of barbarian invasions, with drama and tension reminiscent of frontier conflicts.

7. Sailing The Seas

Evokes Roman maritime journeys, with a sense of adventure and exploration through flowing melodies.

8. Legion

Portrays the discipline and strength of a Roman legion, with martial rhythms and musical cohesion.

9. Festival

Invites the listener to a Roman celebration, capturing cheerful and ceremonial music of public festivities.

10. Eternal

Closes the album with a tribute to the eternity of Rome, evoking historical solemnity and grandeur.
