About Me

Hello, my name is Pablo Núñez Serra. Welcome to my portfolio. I am an Italian–Spanish composer specializing in historical instrumental music, with a particular focus on Ancient Rome.

My work aims to musically recreate different contexts and atmospheres of the Roman world — from civil and religious life to the military sphere — offering an evocative and historically grounded listening experience.

My compositions are conceived as musical accompaniment for museums, historical reenactments, exhibitions, audiovisual presentations, documentaries, films, and other cultural projects.

Below you will find a selection of my works. If you are interested in using my music or commissioning original compositions, please feel free to contact me.
